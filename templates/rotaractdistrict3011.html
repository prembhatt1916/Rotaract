About

Rotaract Club Of College Of Vocational Studies was chartered on 31st August, 2018. It falls under 3011 district, guided by Rotary Club Of Delhi South West and is affiliated by College Of Vocational Studies, University of Delhi. It was the best chartered club in 2018-19. It has been awarded Rotary Citation with Platinum Distinction and is one of the best clubs in the district among 55 clubs. Through various professional, international and community development projects, plantation drives, food donation drives and fellowship events, we persist on doing our part in the Rotaract Movement.

Through the Rotary's motto of "Serve to Change lives", Raccvs continues to strive and do our part to take the banner of Rotaract to greater heights! Our theme for the current session in LEAD- leadership, entrepreneurship, achieve, development, as we'll focus on all these aspects!
