About

Rotary Club Of Delhi South West was chartered under Rotary International Dist 3010, on 1st Oct 1985 by Charter President Rajan Kochhar. It has had 27 past presidents, including the present incumbent, who in their tenure each year have given unique leadership as per their individual strength, capacity, motivation and temperament. Whether, it's a Community Service Project, a Fellowship Dinner, Speaker Meeting or an Out-Station Trip, the bonding between members is amazing
